Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 26th December 2025

Welcome to the Amaanitvam Foundation website. We value the trust you place in us and recognize the importance of secure transactions and information privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how Amaanitvam Foundation collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information about you.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our donors and website users.

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • PAN Card Number (for 80G Tax Exemption receipts)

When you make a donation through our website, payment-related information such as Card Number, CVV, UPI ID, and Banking Details are processed securely by our payment gateway partner. Amaanitvam Foundation does not store any sensitive financial information on its servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process donations and issue receipts.
  • To provide 80G tax exemption certificates where applicable.
  • To communicate regarding donations, events, campaigns, and organizational updates.
  • To improve our website and user experience.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Security

We adopt appropriate security practices and procedures to safeguard personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic.

5. Sharing of Information

Amaanitvam Foundation does not sell, rent, or trade personal information with third parties.

6. Changes to This Policy

Amaanitvam Foundation reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time.
