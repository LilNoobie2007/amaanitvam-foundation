Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find my Certificate ID? expand_more

Your Certificate ID is located in the bottom left corner of your printed or digital certificate. It typically starts with 'AMV-'.

What if my certificate says "Invalid"? expand_more

First, ensure you typed the ID correctly. If it still shows invalid, please contact support immediately as the document may be fraudulent or a data entry error may have occurred.

Can employers verify my certificate directly? expand_more

Yes, anyone with the Certificate ID or QR code can use this portal to instantly verify the authenticity of the credential.

How long are certificates kept on record? expand_more

We maintain digital records indefinitely to ensure your achievements can always be verified.

Need Help With Certificate Verification?

Our support team is available to assist with any verification issues or to report potential fraudulent documents.
